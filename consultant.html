CONSULTANCY

Consultancy

Safeforia provides food safety and quality consulting services to all sectors of the food industry. We can help you develop, implement and maintain food safety and quality management systems for internal requirements and for national and international certifications.

What we do :
  • 1. Developing Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
  • 2. GMP/ GHP documentation and compliance
  • 3. HACCP implementation and compliance
  • 4. Quality/ Food Safety manuals
  • 5. Food safety and quality audits
  • 6. Supplier quality audits
  • 7. GAP analysis/ Pre-audits in preparation for certification audits
  • 8. Regulatory compliance
