ABOUT US

At Safeforia, we understand the nature of food and beverage businesses from farm to plate. This knowledge is based on deep science and rich industry experience. We bring you complete, focussed, sustainable and cost-effective solutions to all your food quality and food safety needs.

Safeforia is strongly committed to good business ethics. We connect our cherished clients with the correct information, targeted services and the right experts to help you produce, manufacture and prepare foods that are safe, compliant and of excellent quality.

Choose Safeforia as your food safety partner to build food safety capacity of your employees and assist you with our diverse range of services that comes conveniently packaged to meet your company needs.

OUR VISION

To empower the food industry with the requisite tools and services that will enable production, manufacture and preparation of safe and quality foods.

OUR MISSION

We inspire, educate and nurture the food industry to deliver uncompromising safe foods always. Every connection with our team is an opportunity to create a memorable experience of excellence that takes the hustle of food safety compliance out of your business. We share our passion in a relaxed and safe environment where client satisfaction is our highest priority.

OUR TEAM

SAFEFORIA TEAM

Dr. Angela Parry-HansonKUNADU

CEO & Lead Consultant
Dr. Angela Parry-Hanson
Angela Parry-Hanson Kunadu (BSc, MSc, PhD) is a proficient Food Scientist and Biotechnologist. She has over 15 years’ experience in the food industry and academia. She started her industry work populating food safety guidelines for Consumer Goods Council for South Africa, then continued as Deputy Quality, Environment, Occupational Health and Safety Manager with Equatorial Coca-Cola Bottling Company for Algeria and West African Countries. She has since played diverse roles providing technical expertise to the food industry and Government institutions. She was instrumental in establishing, implementing and ensuring compliance to Quality, Food Safety, Environment and Occupational Safety and Health Requirements, and Legal requirements in 9 bottling plants in West Africa. Her leadership style, openness, passion and commitment makes her highly successful in driving integrated management systems (ISO 9001, FSSC 22000, ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001) and cultivating compliance culture among diverse teams. She continues to share her passion for food quality and safety through her academic teaching and research, industry training and development, and implementation of various food quality and safety programs. She has expertise in food testing, curricula development and capacity building for all scales of the food industry and government stakeholders. She is a member of the National Codex sub-committee on Food Hygiene and Chaired the Ghana Standards Authority Technical Committee for Microbiological Criteria for Foods.

Dr.Nicole SharonAFFRIFAH

Regulatory Affairs And Factory Design
Dr. Nicole Sharon Affrifah
Dr. Nicole Sharon Affrifah (BSc, MPhil, PhD) is a seasoned Food Scientist with over 20 years of local and international experience gained within both the food industry and academia. She worked for several years in senior management for one of the largest multinational food companies with functional responsibilities for central and West Africa. She currently teaches undergraduate and graduate level courses in Food Process Engineering at the University of Ghana. She continues to provide consultancy services for varied companies and organizations (both local and international). Her areas of expertise include food processing (product/process development or improvement, process flow/layout); standards and compliance (regulatory assessment and compliance strategies for pre-Market approvals, nutritional analysis and labelling, general food labelling compliance); and training (nutrition, food safety and quality, food control) for all staff categories.

Prof. F.K. SAALIA

Chemical Food Safety And Food Processing
Professor Kwesi F. Saalia
Professor Kwesi F. Saalia (BSc, MPhil, PhD) has rich expertise in Food Science and Food Engineering. His career spans over 25 years in food industry support, academia and general management. He has consulted for many food companies and international organizations locally and internationally. His areas of expertise include equipment design and operation, product and process development, food processing, nutraceuticals, toxicology and chemical risk assessments. He has a great personality, loves practical training, and has extensive food policy experience. He chairs several committees at the University of Ghana and the Ghana Standards Authority Technical Committee on Processed Foods, National Codex sub-committee on spices and culinary herbs and National Codex sub-committee on edible oils. He is a Knowledge–Based Partner for British Council on Africa Knowledge transfer Partnerships (AKTPs) with Cocoa Processing Company in the development of (Aspire) sugarless chocolates.

Seth AduBAAH

Partner & Director, Strategic Partnership
Seth Adu Baah
Profile of Seth Adu-Baah EMBA, FCCA, FCISCM, ACIMG Seth has been part of the ECCBC (Equatorial Coca-Cola Bottling Company) team for 20 years, beginning in the Finance Department and progressing to hold various Country Manager roles in Gambia and Liberia, before being promoted to Ghana as Managing Director. Through his excellent leadership, teamwork, resilience, strategic and customer centric approach to business, he has led teams to design and execute programmes that have brought high levels of efficiencies and overall business effectiveness leading to a significant impact to business growth, profitability and return on investment. Prior to his Country Manager appointments, he worked as Management Accountant in Ghana and Head of Finance of the ECCBC operations in Sierra Leone. Seth holds an Executive Master’s degree in Business Administration (Finance option) from the University of Ghana Business School. He is a Fellow of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA), UK and Chartered Institute of Supply Chain Management, Ghana, and an Associate member of Chartered Institute of Marketing, Ghana, with various qualifications in leadership, Business Management and Entrepreneurship from London School of Economics, Cranfield University, School of Management, AT EMILE Woolf College, London; San Telmo Business School, Seville-Spain; ESADE Business School, Barcelona Spain, and Wits Business School, South Africa. In February 2018, he was appointed to the position of Managing Director – The Coca-Cola Bottling Company of Ghana Limited, the first Ghanaian to hold this position in the company. Seth has served on many Boards in both private and public organizations as well as government committee. Notably but to mention a few is the key role played as Chairman of the Local Organizing Committee of the FIFA/Coca-Cola World Cup Trophy Tour. Seth is currently serving on J-Palm Liberia Ltd as a Board Member, and Board Member, Junior Achievers Africa.
